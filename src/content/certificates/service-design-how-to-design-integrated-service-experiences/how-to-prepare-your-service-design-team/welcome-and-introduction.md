## Lesson 2: Scoping Service Design Projects

### Lesson Overview

- Service design project scope: Define boundaries, goals, and alignment with the organization.
- Organizational support: Ensure stakeholders and teams are aligned with the design process.

### Business Alignment Tools

#### Business Model Canvas

- Purpose: Ensure the service fits the business model.
- Benefits:
  - Clarifies how the service creates value.
  - Aligns service design with business goals.

#### Value Proposition Canvas

- Purpose: Map the value created for users and the business.
- Focus:
  - Customer needs and expectations.
  - How the service delivers value effectively.

### Common Challenges in Service Design

- Misalignment: Between business goals and user needs.
- Complexity: Managing multiple stakeholders and touchpoints.
- Collaboration gaps: Lack of communication across teams.

### Collaboration & Co-Creation

- Co-creation: Involve stakeholders and users in the design process.
- Collaboration: Essential skill for service designers to ensure shared understanding and better outcomes.

### Workshops in Service Design

- Workshops: Structured sessions to:
  - Align teams
  - Generate ideas
  - Map processes and dependencies

### Learning Outcome

- Scope projects collaboratively: Define clear boundaries with stakeholders.
- Prepare for execution: Ready to use tools to map systems, interdependencies, and service processes.
