## What You Learned

- Service design requires **clear scoping and alignment** across the organization
- Focus on combining:
  - User needs
  - Business goals
  - Collaborative processes

## Key Tools

### Business Model Canvas

- Understand how the service fits the business
- Align value creation with business strategy

### Value Proposition Canvas

- Define how the service creates value
- Match:
  - Customer needs (jobs, pains, gains)
  - Service offerings (solutions, benefits)

## Defining the Right Problem

- Always identify the **right problem before designing solutions**
- Focus on:
  - Root causes (not symptoms)
  - Real user needs

## Business Context First

- Start with **business objectives**
- Then design solutions that:
  - Deliver value
  - Support long-term goals

## Collaboration & Co-Creation

- Service design is a **team effort**
- Involves:
  - Stakeholders
  - Cross-functional teams
  - Users
- Benefits:
  - Better insights
  - Stronger alignment
  - Higher success rate

## Workshops as a Core Method

- Workshops help:
  - Align teams
  - Generate ideas
  - Map systems and processes
- Require:
  - Good facilitation
  - Clear structure
  - Active participation

## Key Takeaways

- Don’t jump into solutions → **define the problem first**
- Align:
  - User needs
  - Business goals
  - Team collaboration
- Use:
  - Canvases for structure
  - Workshops for alignment
  - Co-creation for better outcomes
- Result:
  - More strategic, scalable, and effective service design
