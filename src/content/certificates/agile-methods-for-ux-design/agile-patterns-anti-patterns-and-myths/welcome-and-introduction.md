## Persistent Myths and Realities of Agile

### Lesson Focus

- Patterns: Common characteristics found in truly agile teams.
- Anti-patterns: Misinterpretations or dysfunctional implementations of agile practices.
- Myths: Widespread but incorrect beliefs about agile methodologies.
- Team structures: How agile teams are organized in real-world contexts.

### What You Will Learn

- Common patterns and anti-patterns:
  - How to recognize healthy agile behaviors.
  - How to identify teams that misunderstand or misuse agile.

- Why anti-patterns exist:
  - The myths that sustain them.
  - Practical strategies to address these misconceptions.

- Agile and team size:
  - Why agile works better in smaller teams.
  - How large organizations adapt agile differently.

- Large-scale frameworks:
  - SAFe
  - Scrum of Scrums

- Implications for UX:
  - How patterns, anti-patterns, myths, and structures affect UX designers and researchers.
