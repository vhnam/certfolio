## Build with AI: Expert Advice Using Gems

### Lab Preparation

- Gemini login: Sign in using a Google Account.
- Prompt customization: Adjust prompts to reflect your own role, industry, and challenges.
- Learning goal: Use Gems to generate ideas, gain new perspectives, and share expert knowledge.

### Step 1: Explore an Expert Persona

Identify the traits of a relevant expert.

Example prompt:

```
What are the key characteristics of [an expert role relevant to your work, e.g. a highly competent interior designer]?
```

Purpose:

- Understand the expertise, skills, and mindset of a professional role.
- Use the generated characteristics as the foundation for building a Gem.

### Step 2: Create a Custom Gem

Create a personalized AI expert inside Gemini.

Process:

1. Open the Gemini menu.
2. Select **Gems** and go to **Gem Manager**.
3. Click **New Gem**.
4. Provide a name and description for the Gem.
5. Add instructions defining the expert persona.

Example instruction template:

```
You are [expert role, e.g. a highly competent interior designer]. Your key characteristics include: [characteristics from Gemini’s output in the previous step]. I am a [your role]. Use your expertise to challenge my thinking and ensure my ideas are sound. Your advice should be clear, candid, and informative. Stay in character throughout the conversation.
```

Purpose:

- Simulate an expert advisor tailored to your work context.
- Provide critical feedback and professional insights.

### Step 3: Ask Your Gem Questions

Interact with the custom expert to get advice.

Example question:

```
What are the top factors to consider in selecting a [product or service offered by your company]?
```

Possible uses:

- Get expert advice
- Evaluate ideas
- Validate strategies
- Answer domain-specific questions

### Sharing and Collaboration

- Gem sharing: Gems can be shared through **Explore Gems**.
- Team benefit: Teams can reuse expert Gems for consistent advice and insights.

### Next Steps

- Create additional Gems for different expert roles.
- Use Gems to support decision-making and idea validation in your work.
- Identify which expert perspectives would be most useful for you or your team.

## Using Multiple Gems as a Personal AI Team

### Personal Workflow with Gems

- AI team concept: Gems can function like a small team of specialized assistants.
- Role specialization: Each Gem focuses on a specific responsibility or expertise.

### Project Manager Gem

- Project coordination: One Gem acts as a project manager.
- Task organization: Helps structure tasks and decide which Gem should handle them.
- Gem creation support: Assists in generating new specialized Gems when needed.

### “Gem-erator” Concept

- Gem-erator: A Gem designed to help create other Gems.
- Purpose: Generate new AI experts for niche or task-specific needs.
- Automation: Simplifies the process of building specialized AI assistants.

### Specialized Task Gems

Examples of niche Gems:

- Ad copy Gem: Focuses on writing advertising copy.
- Messaging Gem: Helps craft messaging and communication strategies.

Customization options:

- Specific documentation style
- Particular tone of voice
- Task-focused expertise

### Benefits of a Gem-Based Workflow

- Clear task delegation: Assign different tasks to specialized Gems.
- Efficient workflows: Quickly create AI experts for specific needs.
- Flexible collaboration: Manage multiple AI assistants like a coordinated team.
