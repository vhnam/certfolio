## Build with AI: Research Report

### Lab Preparation

- Gemini login: Sign in to Gemini using a Google Account.
- Prompt customization: Adjust example prompts to match your own work or research topic.
- Video guidance: Follow the lab video and pause when necessary while completing steps.

### Step 1: Get Initial Market Trend Ideas

Start by asking Gemini for broad insights.

Example prompt:

```
I'm planning a [new product or service, e.g. new paint line] for my company. What [information, e.g. risks or market trends] should I be aware of?
```

Purpose:

- Generate initial ideas about risks, trends, or opportunities.
- Use the response as a starting point for deeper research.

### Step 2: Start a Deep Research Project

Use Gemini’s **Deep Research** tool to analyze a specific trend.

Process:

1. Start a new chat in Gemini.
2. Select **Deep Research** from the Tools menu.
3. Use a prompt based on a trend identified earlier.

Example prompt:

```
I'm planning a [new product or service]. Research [specific market trend].
```

Result:

- Gemini generates a **Research Plan** outlining how it will investigate the topic.

### Step 3: Refine the Research Plan

Improve the research focus before running the analysis.

Example prompt:

```
Focus on [specific aspect of the topic], use only sources from [time frame], and include [specific data points].
```

Customization options:

- Narrow the research scope
- Limit the time range of sources
- Require specific metrics or datasets

### Step 4: Run the Research

- Click **Start Research** in the Deep Research chat.
- Gemini collects and analyzes information from multiple sources.
- A structured **research report** is generated after a few minutes.

### Step 5: Generate an Infographic

Transform the research report into a visual summary.

Process:

1. Open the completed research report.
2. Click **Create** in the top-right corner.
3. Select **Infographic**.

Benefits:

- Converts complex findings into easy-to-understand visuals
- Makes insights easier to share with others
