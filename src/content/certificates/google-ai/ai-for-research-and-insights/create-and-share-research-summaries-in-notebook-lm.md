## Build with AI: Research Summaries with NotebookLM

### Lab Preparation

- NotebookLM login: Sign in using a Google Account.
- Prompt customization: Adjust prompts to match your own work or research topic.
- Video guidance: Follow the lab video and pause when necessary.

### Step 1: Generate a Summary of Your Sources

Create a notebook and upload research materials.

Process:

1. Select **Create a new notebook** in NotebookLM.
2. Upload documents or media sources.
3. Enter a summarization prompt.

Example prompt:

```
Summarize the key learnings from these sources in [desired length].
```

Supported source types:

- Podcast
- PDF documents
- Websites
- YouTube videos

Key feature:

- Citation-based summaries: NotebookLM links the summary directly to the original sources, helping verify accuracy and reduce hallucinations.

### Step 2: Ask Questions About Your Sources

Interact with the uploaded materials through questions.

Capabilities:

- Identify themes across multiple documents.
- Explore details within a specific source.
- Locate which documents discuss certain topics.

Example question:

```
Do any of these sources talk about how to test different color choices in design?
```

### Step 3: Generate Additional Resources

Use the **Studio panel** to transform your sources into different formats.

Available outputs:

- Audio Overview: Spoken summary of the content.
- Video Overview: Visual explanation of key insights.
- Mind Map: Visual structure of topics and relationships.

### Benefits of Using NotebookLM

- Faster understanding: Quickly extract insights from multiple files.
- Reliable outputs: Answers are grounded in the uploaded sources.
- Flexible formats: Information can be transformed into multiple presentation styles.

### Next Steps

- Upload documents related to your own work or projects.
- Use NotebookLM to better understand complex information collections.
- Select sources responsibly and verify outputs when sharing insights.

## Using NotebookLM Audio Overviews for Research

### Primary Use Case

- Audio overviews: Convert long research documents into spoken summaries.
- Hands-free learning: Listen to summaries while doing other activities, such as walking.

### Handling Large Research Materials

- Long documents: Research reports and PDFs can range from 20 to 100+ pages.
- Faster consumption: Upload documents to NotebookLM to quickly understand the main ideas.

### Interactive Research

- Question-based exploration: Ask follow-up questions about the content.
- Deeper understanding: Clarify details or explore specific parts of the research.

### Benefits

- Efficient learning: Absorb complex information without reading entire documents.
- Flexible consumption: Listen to summaries instead of reading long files.
- Enhanced insight: Combine audio summaries with interactive questions.
