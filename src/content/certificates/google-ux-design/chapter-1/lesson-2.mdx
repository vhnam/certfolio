## Roles in UX

UX is a broad field with many specializations. Some common roles include:

| Role | Focus |
|------|-------|
| Interaction Designer | Micro-interactions, task flows |
| Visual Designer | Typography, color, layout |
| Motion Designer | Animation and transitions |
| UX Researcher | User interviews, usability tests |
| UX Writer | Microcopy, content strategy |
| Product Designer | End-to-end ownership |

## Cross-functional collaboration

UX designers regularly collaborate with product managers, engineers, and stakeholders. Understanding how these roles complement each other is essential for effective design work.

## Key takeaway

Knowing the landscape of UX roles helps you identify where your strengths fit and how to communicate your value to teams.
