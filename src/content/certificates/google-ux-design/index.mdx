## About this certificate

The Google UX Design Professional Certificate is a 7-course program that covers the foundations of UX design — from conducting user research through prototyping and usability testing.

**Completed:** October 31, 2025

## What I learned

- The end-to-end UX design process (empathize → define → ideate → prototype → test)
- How to conduct and synthesize user research
- Wireframing and prototyping in Figma
- Accessibility and inclusive design principles
- Building a professional UX portfolio
