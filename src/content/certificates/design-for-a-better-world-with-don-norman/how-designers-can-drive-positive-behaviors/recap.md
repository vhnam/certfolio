## Key Learnings

### Human Resistance to Change

- Behavioral Resistance: People tend to avoid change.
- Tangible Impact: Action is more likely when consequences are visible or felt.

### Role of Human Behavior

- Core Factor: Human behavior is central to solving climate change and global challenges.
- Behavioral Influence: Lasting solutions depend on changing how people act.

### Designers as Change Agents

- Expanded Role: Designers can drive impact beyond climate change.
- Cross-Domain Impact: Opportunities in education, healthcare, and other societal issues.

## What’s Next

### Future of Design

- Evolving Field: Design continues to expand into complex global challenges.
- Skill Leverage: Designers can apply their unique capabilities for broader impact.

### Key Focus Areas Ahead

- Collaboration: Working across disciplines to solve complex problems.
- Strategy: Approaching challenges with systems thinking.
- Technology: Leveraging emerging tools to enhance design impact.
