title: "What are the first steps to design a better future?"
---

## Finding the Right Group to Collaborate With

### Key Criteria

- Alignment with skills:
  - Choose groups where your design abilities are useful.
- Openness to design:
  - Join groups that value and accept design methods.
- Fit with interests:
  - Select groups that match your passions and goals.

### Types of Groups

- Public organizations
- Private initiatives
- Government groups
- Community-driven projects

## Learning Through Exploration

### Curiosity-Driven Approach

- Be curious:
  - Actively explore real-world problems.
- Observe communities:
  - Identify issues like:
    - Lack of transportation
    - Poor public services
    - Social inequality

### Joining Existing Efforts

- Look for ongoing initiatives:
  - Many groups already work on societal problems.
- Contribute and lead:
  - Support solutions using design skills.

## Driving Change Within Organizations

### Applying Design in Companies

- Advocate for change:
  - Push for more sustainable and responsible practices.
- Influence strategy:
  - Use design thinking to reshape how businesses operate.

### Creating New Business Models

- Shift from product to service:
  - Example:
    - Instead of selling products, offer outcomes or services.
- Sustainability focus:
  - Reduce environmental impact.
- Business viability:
  - Ensure profitability while being responsible.

#### Examples

- Tire company model:
  - Sell “tire performance service” instead of tires.
  - Provide maintenance, replacement, and monitoring.
- Jet engine model:
  - Sell “guaranteed power” instead of engines.
  - Handle maintenance and predictive repairs.

## Designers’ Role in Systemic Change

### Core Contributions

- Creativity:
  - Develop innovative solutions and business models.
- Systems thinking:
  - Rethink how industries operate.
- Collaboration:
  - Work across disciplines and sectors.

## Takeaways

- First step:
  - Find and join the right group aligned with your skills.
- Second step:
  - Explore real-world problems and engage with communities.
- Organizational impact:
  - Redesign business models for sustainability and impact.
- Ultimate goal:
  - Create solutions that are sustainable, equitable, and profitable.
