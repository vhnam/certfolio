## Lesson Overview

- Future of design: Focuses on leveraging new technologies to create positive societal impact.
- Designers as agents of change: Designers play an active role in addressing real-world problems and shaping a better future.

## Key Learning Objectives

- Designers’ role in society:
  - Designers have the skills to help solve societal problems.
- Community-focused strategies:
  - Apply design methods to address issues within local communities.
- Collaboration:
  - Partner with existing groups working on complex challenges to increase impact.
- Emerging technologies:
  - Simplify design processes.
  - Create new and exciting job opportunities.

## Practical Outcomes

- Case study completion:
  - Learn how to refine (finetune) your work.
  - Prepare and submit a complete case study effectively.
