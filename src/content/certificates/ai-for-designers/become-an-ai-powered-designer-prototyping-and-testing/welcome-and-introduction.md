## Overview of AI in Design Workflow

- AI-enabled tools: Integrated into design teams to improve efficiency and speed.
- Workflow integration: Requires experimentation to find the right fit in your process.
- Designer responsibility: Critically evaluate outputs and adapt tools effectively.

## AI Tools for Prototyping

### Wireframing

- AI for wireframing: Generates layout structures and early concepts quickly.
- Efficiency: Reduces time spent on initial drafts.

### Visual Design

- AI in visual design: Assists in creating UI elements and visual compositions.
- Iteration: Enables rapid exploration of multiple design directions.

### UX Writing

- AI for UX writing: Produces microcopy, labels, and content suggestions.
- Consistency: Helps maintain tone and clarity across the product.

## AI Tools for Evaluation

- Design evaluation: AI supports testing and analyzing usability.
- Feedback generation: Identifies potential issues and areas for improvement.

## AI for Production Readiness

- Grunt work automation: Handles repetitive and time-consuming tasks.
- Launch support: Accelerates preparation for product release.

## Key Mindset for Using AI

- Tool mindset: Use AI as support, not replacement.
- Critical thinking: Always review and validate AI outputs.
- Continuous experimentation: Explore and refine how AI fits your workflow.
