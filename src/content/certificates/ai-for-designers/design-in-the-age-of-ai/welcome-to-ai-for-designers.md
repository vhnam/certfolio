## Introduction

This course introduces how designers can integrate Artificial Intelligence (AI) into their design practice. You’ll learn both how to **design AI-powered products** and how to **use AI as a collaborative tool** within your workflow.

Rather than replacing designers, AI can act as a powerful partner that helps you work faster, explore ideas, and solve problems more effectively. Throughout the course, you’ll gain a practical understanding of AI concepts, tools, and design considerations so you can confidently apply them in your work.

The goal is to help you build a **strong foundation for working with AI**, understand its opportunities and challenges, and develop the mindset needed to shape responsible and meaningful AI-driven products.

## Course at a Glance

The course explores how AI is transforming design and why designers should actively engage with it. As Ioana emphasizes:

> **“AI won't replace designers. A designer using AI will.”**

AI is evolving quickly, and designers who learn to work with it will be better positioned to shape how it influences products and workflows.

The course is structured into four main lessons:

**Lesson 1 — AI Foundations**

- Understand key AI concepts such as **Machine Learning**, **Deep Learning**, and **Generative AI**
- Explore how AI is influencing the design industry
- Learn how to craft effective prompts for AI tools

**Lesson 2 — AI in the Design Workflow**

- Use AI for **UX research**, including market analysis and persona creation
- Analyze data and gain insights more efficiently
- Improve problem-solving with AI-assisted tools

**Lesson 3 — AI for Design and Prototyping**

- Use AI for **visual design, prototyping, and UX writing**
- Evaluate designs with AI assistance
- Automate repetitive design tasks

**Lesson 4 — Designing Responsible AI**

- Understand the challenges of AI-driven products
- Address ethical considerations and risks
- Ensure AI-powered solutions remain human-centered

Each lesson also includes additional references and resources so you can deepen your understanding beyond the course material.

## Build Your Portfolio

Throughout the course, you’ll complete **“Build Your Portfolio” activities** designed to help you apply what you learn in practical scenarios.

These exercises guide you through real design tasks using AI tools so you can gradually build a **case study demonstrating your AI design skills**.

You can complete these activities in two ways:

- **Alongside each lesson** as you progress through the course
- **After finishing the course**, returning to the exercises later

Experimenting with different AI tools is encouraged. By testing multiple tools and approaches, you’ll learn how to integrate AI effectively into your own design workflow.

To make the most of these activities, be sure to **document your entire process**, as this will make it easier to build a strong portfolio case study.

## What You’ll Need

You can complete the course using any device connected to the internet, but a **desktop or laptop** will provide a smoother experience when working with larger amounts of content.

Some AI tools may require you to create an account, but many offer **free tiers or trial versions**, allowing you to explore their capabilities without cost.

## Conclusion

AI is rapidly reshaping how designers work. By learning how to collaborate with AI tools and understand AI-driven products, you can expand your capabilities and future-proof your design career.

This course will help you develop the **skills, mindset, and practical experience** needed to use AI confidently and responsibly in your design process.
