## Introduction

Lesson 1 introduced the fundamentals of artificial intelligence and its growing impact on design. The lesson emphasized how designers can work with AI as a **collaborative partner** to improve their design process and productivity.

Throughout the lesson, the focus was on understanding AI concepts and learning how to communicate effectively with AI systems.

## Key Topics Covered

### Importance of AI

- Artificial Intelligence is transforming industries and workflows.
- Designers can use AI to enhance creativity, efficiency, and decision-making.

Understanding AI is becoming an **essential skill for modern designers**.

### Core AI Concepts

The lesson introduced several key concepts related to AI:

- Artificial Intelligence (AI): The broad field of building intelligent systems.
- Machine Learning: A subset of AI that enables systems to learn from data.
- Deep Learning: A subset of machine learning that uses neural networks.

These concepts form the **foundation of modern AI technologies**.

### Generative AI

- Generative AI: AI systems capable of creating new content such as text, images, or media.

Applications include:

- Content generation
- Image creation
- Creative assistance

Generative AI enables designers to **generate ideas and accelerate creative workflows**.

### AI as a Design Partner

The lesson emphasized that AI should be viewed as a **partner rather than a replacement** for designers.

Benefits of collaboration with AI include:

- Faster workflows
- Improved idea generation
- Support for research and analysis

Human skills such as **critical thinking and creativity remain essential**.

### Prompt Engineering

- Prompt: The instruction or input given to an AI system.
- Prompt engineering: Designing prompts effectively to guide AI outputs.

Learning how to craft clear and structured prompts helps designers achieve **better and more accurate results** from AI tools.

## What Comes Next

The next part of the course will focus on **how to integrate AI into the design process**.

Now that the foundational concepts of AI and prompt communication are clear, the following lessons will explore practical ways to apply AI tools in real design workflows.
