## What You’ve Learned

### AI as a Workflow Accelerator

- AI improves efficiency by:
  - Speeding up research tasks
  - Reducing manual effort
  - Helping process large datasets

→ Enables faster movement from **research → insight → action**

### AI Across UX Research Activities

- AI supports:
  - Market research
  - Competitive analysis
  - AI-generated persona interviews
  - Data processing and summarization

→ Useful for **exploration and early insights**, not final validation

### AI in Problem-Solving and Ideation

- Helps:
  - Frame problems
  - Generate ideas
  - Explore solution spaces

→ Expands creativity, but requires **human judgment to refine**

## Understanding AI Trade-offs

### Advantages

- Speed and scalability
- Rapid idea generation
- Efficient data processing

### Disadvantages

- Lack of real context
- Risk of bias and hallucination
- Generic or shallow outputs

→ AI is powerful but **not reliable on its own**

## The Role of Critical Thinking

- Always:
  - Evaluate outputs
  - Question assumptions
  - Fact-check information

→ AI outputs = **starting points, not truth**

## What’s Next

### Moving into Prototyping and Testing

- Next step in the workflow:
  - Use AI to assist with:
    - Prototyping
    - Testing

→ Focus shifts from:

- Ideas → **validation and execution**

## Final Insight

- AI enhances speed and exploration
- Designers ensure:
  - Accuracy
  - relevance
  - meaningful outcomes

→ The best results come from combining **AI efficiency with human critical thinking**
