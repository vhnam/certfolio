## The Problem: Too Much Data, Too Little Time

- User research produces:
  - Hours of recordings
  - Large volumes of notes
- Manual analysis is:
  - Time-consuming
  - Mentally exhausting

→ AI helps compress **weeks of work into hours** by processing large datasets quickly.

## What AI Does Best in Research

### Transcription & Summarization

- Transcribe and translate interviews
- Generate quick summaries of key points

⚠️ Always:

- Double-check transcripts (small errors change meaning)
- Use tools with **citations** to trace insights

### Thematic Analysis (Coding)

- Cluster similar responses
- Identify recurring themes and keywords

→ Useful as a **first pass**, not final structure  
→ Requires human refinement for nuance

### Sentiment & Pattern Detection

- Analyze large datasets (surveys, transcripts)
- Detect:
  - Positive/negative sentiment
  - Frequently mentioned issues

→ Treat outputs as **signals**, not conclusions

## How to Work Effectively with AI

### Define Clear Goals

- Be specific about what you want:
  - Pain points
  - Patterns
  - Feature feedback

→ Clear input = relevant output

### Use Critical Thinking

- AI may:
  - Misinterpret meaning
  - Miss context
  - Hallucinate insights

→ Always verify against raw data

### Triangulate Data

- Cross-check AI outputs with:
  - Original transcripts
  - Other research sources
  - Team discussions

→ Especially important when using AI-generated data

## The Human Role in Research

### AI = Junior Analyst

- Strength: Speed, scale, pattern detection
- Weakness: No real understanding or context

→ Designers must:

- Interpret meaning
- Prioritize insights
- Make decisions

### Collaboration Still Matters

- AI-only analysis removes:
  - Team perspective
  - Direct exposure to user quotes

→ Human collaboration:

- Builds empathy
- Produces deeper insights

### Protect User Privacy

- Remove personal data before using AI tools
- Follow:
  - Consent agreements
  - Data protection policies

## Key Takeaways

- AI accelerates:
  - Transcription
  - Theming
  - Pattern detection
- But requires:
  - Clear goals
  - Verification
  - Human judgment

### Final Insight

- AI processes data
- Humans create meaning

→ The best outcomes come from combining **AI speed with human insight**
