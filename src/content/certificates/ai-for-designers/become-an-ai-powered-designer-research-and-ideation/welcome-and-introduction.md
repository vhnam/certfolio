## Lesson Overview

### Scope

- AI in Product Design: Application across the full workflow from research to defining requirements.
- Instructor Context: Insights from Ioana Teleanu (UX Goodies, ex-Miro AI Lead Product Designer).
- Core Focus: Practical usage of AI tools while acknowledging their limitations.

## AI-Enhanced Workflow

### Research with AI

#### Market Research & Competitive Analysis

- Market research: Use AI to quickly gather industry insights and trends.
- Competitive analysis: Analyze competitors’ features, positioning, and gaps.

#### AI-Generated Personas

- AI personas: Simulated users for conducting mock interviews.
- Interview simulation: Generate responses to explore user needs and behaviors.

#### Data Processing

- Data analysis: Summarize and extract insights from large datasets.
- Pattern detection: Identify trends and key findings efficiently.

### Problem Definition

- Problem statement: Use AI to refine and articulate clear problem definitions.
- Insight synthesis: Convert research findings into actionable design directions.

### Ideation with AI

- Production ideation: Generate design ideas, concepts, and solutions rapidly.
- Exploration: Expand design possibilities beyond initial assumptions.

## Limitations and Considerations

### AI Constraints

- Limitations of AI: Outputs may lack real-world accuracy or context.
- Bias & reliability: AI-generated insights require validation.

### Human-Centered Validation

- Real user research: Essential for validating assumptions and insights.
- Balanced approach: Combine AI efficiency with human-centered design practices.
