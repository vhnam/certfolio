## Applying the Gestalt Laws

### Law of Common Fate

- Law of Common Fate: Humans perceive elements that are moving or changing together as belonging to the same group.
- Gestalt Psychologists: Max Wertheimer, Kurt Koffka, Wolfgang Köhler.
- Common fate cues:
  - Shared movement
  - Shared direction
